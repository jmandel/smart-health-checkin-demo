SMART Health Check-in

Two demos, one protocol
🏥

Patient Portal

Patient is on their own device. They tap a button, a popup opens, they select their health app and share data.

Same-Device Flow
🖥️

Front Desk Kiosk

Staff starts the check-in at a workstation. A QR code appears. The patient scans it on their phone to share data.

Cross-Device Flow

Both demos use the same protocol stack: well_known: verifier identity, signed Request Objects, direct_post.jwt, and the same wallet/picker/source apps.
